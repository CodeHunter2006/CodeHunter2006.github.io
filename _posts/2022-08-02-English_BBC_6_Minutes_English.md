# short spoken forms

- These short forms always used in informal spoken english. You'd better not use them in formal place, like in a job interview.

- `whatcha`
  - what are you
  - what do you
- `wanna`
  - want to
- `dunno`
  - don't know
- `gonna`
  - going to
- `gotta`
  - got to
  - got a
- `kinda`
  - kind of
- `lemme`
  - let me
- `hafta`
  - have to
- `gimme`
  - give me
